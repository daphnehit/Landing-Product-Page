There are a whole lot of serums out there, from eyelash serum to hyaluronic acid serum to anti-ageing face serum. But what if you only have a room in your makeup bag for one? Experts say to go with vitamin C serum because it works.'Vitamin C is powerful antioxidant that fights fine lines by stimulating collagen, brightens your complexion by blocking excess pigment creation, and protects the skin from damaging free radical and other environmental stressors.' says Michelle Henry, clinical instructor of dermatology at Weill Cornell Medical College. 'As we age,though, the skin's natural levels of antioxidant vitamin c decrease, which is why it's important to add it to your skin routine.' The one downside? Compared to most products, it often cost more per ounce.

Different types of vitamin C serum

Plus, when you start waking up to brighter, smoother skin everyday, you'll realize it's well worth the price.

With continued use, Silymarin CF will improve your skin tone and texture, as well as reduce fine lines, wrinkles, and excess sebum production.

The name La Roche-Posay's Vitamin C Serum is as straightforward as it gets, unmistakely touting the formula's 10-percent ascorbic acid.
